For Donors

Step 1. Write-up a brief summary of your donation.

Step 2. Deposit fund on the site.

Step 3. Once your donation is matched, your funds will be paid out to the matcher.

For Matchers

Step 1. Find a commitment that your company can match.

Step 2. Using your own funds, donate to the organization referenced in the commitment.

Step 3. Submit confirmation of the match to Multifire, and receive funds from the donor.
